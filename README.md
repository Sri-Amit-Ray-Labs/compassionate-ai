---

## Vision

Artificial intelligence should not only become more intelligent—it should become more compassionate.

Compassionate AI seeks to integrate scientific excellence, ethical responsibility, transparency, empathy, wisdom, and human values into intelligent systems that benefit individuals, communities, future generations, and the planet.

Our vision is to build AI that contributes to:

- Human flourishing
- International peace
- Ethical governance
- Human dignity
- Responsible innovation
- Sustainable development
- Global cooperation
- Trustworthy human–AI collaboration

---

# Mission

The mission of this repository is to provide an open ecosystem for researchers, developers, educators, organizations, and policymakers working to create AI systems that are not only powerful but also compassionate, fair, transparent, safe, and beneficial.

---

# What is Compassionate AI?

Compassionate AI is an emerging paradigm that extends traditional AI beyond accuracy and efficiency.

It combines:

- Artificial Intelligence
- Ethics
- Compassion
- Human-centered design
- Explainability
- Fairness
- Transparency
- Safety
- Human flourishing
- Peace-oriented decision making
- Responsible governance

Compassion becomes a measurable engineering objective rather than merely a philosophical ideal.
